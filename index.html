About

Devenire is a company focused in software development, design & business solutions and multimedia content. It was created early in 2017 with the raw talent of programmers, designers, audio designers and marketeers. We work very hard for our clients to be satisfied, always looking forward to provide the best service through the hands of our great team.

PROJECTS

FreshBoard

There is a ever growing need for new solutions for dealing with stock management, ensuring stability through the many market fluctuations of offer, demand, availability and other external factors. So, it is essential to develop tools equipped with machine learning in order to manage all these variables and devenire will be there to innovate in this sector.

Surgeon Mate Library

Founded in UPTEC’s incubator, this start-up provide surgeons a new tool to record, archive and share multimedia files from their many procedures. Having the aid of a wearable glasses device, surgeons can review surgeries thorugh an easy-to-use, secure and anonymous platform that allows them to give the proper feedback for further improvements.

Invoice Capture

In the business world it became harder to manage all the clients accounts and invoices. So, InvoiceCapture was created in order to solve and organize these problems, recording customers’ requests, calculate different amounts and allow the customer to negotiate and notify everyone properly. All together in a single automated and frictionless platform.
