devenire

Company

Devenire is a company focused in software development, design & multimedia contents and business solutions. It was created early in 2017 with the raw talent put together with the ambition of creating an impact the world. Every project brings a new challenge and devenire brings to the table a customized solution for each one, going from the code to the promotion of the final product. Currently, devenire is working with four different clients in the tech industry, looking forward to bring innovative products to each one.


Retail Forecast (Sonae-BIT)

There’s an ever growing need for new solutions for dealing with stock management, assuring stability through the many fluctuations in demand, availability, sales offers and other external factors. So, it is essential to develop tools equipped with machine learning in order to manage all these variables. The company’s role here is to identify these instability factors and how to respond with sucess to each one of them.

SurgeonMate Library (SurgeonMate)

This startup is currently incubated in UPTEC and it has as its main product a visual device that helps surgeons to record, archive and share multimedia files from their many procedures. This interface will be ease-to-use, grounded in the principles of ethics, security and allowing a surgeon to show his techniques to others for constant improvement, evaluation and educational purposes.

InvoiceCapture (Invisible Cloud)

Defined by its innovation, this tech company tackles the online management of invoices. Its main product being developed with devenire’s is InvoiceCapture, which can record customer’s request, calculate the different amounts and offers and then notify the answer to them. Everything done frictionlessly in a automated process.

Coding@escolaglobal® (escolaglobal®)

This private school institution wants to improve its offer of coding classes for students from 1st to 9th grade, bringing new exercises and contents in python, blocky and robots programming. This new classes will be part of a local platform that will be installed in its students computers for working at school and at home.
